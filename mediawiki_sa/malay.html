Malay is a group of humans that usually live in Malaysia, Singapore, Brunei Darussalam. Malay language is fourth largest language in the world. Countries that Malay language is used widely include Brunei, Malaysia, Indonesia, The Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Australia, China and Saudi Arabia.

Malay language or Bahasa Melayu have some words that same as other language like:

Sayang - (Tagalok) Wang - (Hokkien)
